How the motivational interview is conducted?

During the motivational interview, which is conducted in English, the candidate is asked to:
1. Provide a brief presentation of their curriculum vitae;
2. Illustrate the professional motivations behind the application;
3. Describe the goals they set for their career development following the course;
4. Illustrate the technologies they are interested in;
5. Specify the application areas in which they believe they can develop their activity within the program;
6. Provide a description of their personal and professional characteristics;
7. Provide a description of their creative skills.
